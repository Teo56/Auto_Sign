Hello! We are a group of Imperial College Bioengineering students that created SignTube to make sign language more accessible and easy to learn. With SignTube, you can get all your favorite Youtube videos translated to sign language! All you have to do is copy paste the Youtube URL and click on SignTube!


We are constantly working on improving our platform to make it easier to use and more flexible for our beloved users!


A special thanks to the PaceCenter for giving us all the guidance and insight to make this project achievable!
